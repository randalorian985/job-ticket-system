# UI-006-01: Office Role Task Validation

## Status
Planning child of UI-006.

## Goal
Validate approved shell, work-order, and operations concepts against real Manager, Dispatcher, Reviewer, and Administrator tasks.

## Dependencies
Requires completed UI-001 through UI-004.

## Scope
- Run task walkthroughs for finding/creating/reopening work orders, assignment/scheduling, blocked and overdue work, time approval, parts processing, reporting, master data, and administration.
- Record hesitation, wrong destinations, missed status, context loss, terminology confusion, inaccessible controls, and permission-visibility errors.
- Validate desktop, tablet, and manager-mobile concepts.
- Return defects to the owning UI parent for revision and rerun affected tasks.
- Produce outcome, severity, and resolution records.

## Required artifacts
- Office-role task-validation matrix.
- Observation and outcome notes.
- Revised wireframe references and unresolved blocker list.

## Acceptance criteria
- Critical office tasks complete without hidden knowledge of internal IDs or module names.
- Critical findability, context, accessibility, and permission failures are resolved or block approval explicitly.
- Each task records tested role, viewport, result, finding, and disposition.

## Guardrail
Do not validate technician execution here and do not change production UI.
